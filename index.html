Web Designer
One Firefly 2 review
Philadelphia, PA

Job Title: Web Designer

Reports to: Web Team Manager
Location: Remote (Full-Time, Work-from-home position)

POSITION SUMMARY:

The Web Designer will be principally tasked with the management and deployment of all client-facing web efforts all while providing exceptional client experience. Reporting to the Web Team Manager, this person will play an integral role in the production of the organization’s web-related product offerings while also making significant contributions to One Firefly’s growth and efficiency.

Responsibilities include (but are not limited to):

  • Produce One Firefly Design Model websites for clients
  • Communicate with One Firefly clients throughout project lifecycles, always keeping them apprised of project status
  • Satisfy Web Maintenance Service requests and complete work-orders per SOW
  • Setup and configure blogs on existing WordPress and Joomla websites
  • SEO configure client websites, and perform a variety of SEO related tasks
  • Perform basic graphic editing tasks as necessary; format, crop, resize images, etc.
  • Perform basic server maintenance and DNS management
  • Assist the department with internal web-related initiatives as necessary

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in related discipline strongly preferred.
  • Exceptional communication skills with both internal staff and clients
  • Highly experienced using website Content Management Systems
  • Basic experience using Joomla CMS preferred, but not required
  • Basic HTML and Basic CSS knowledge preferred
  • Basic PHP knowledge preferred, but not required
  • Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator & Dreamweaver experience preferred
  • Familiarity with server cPanel strongly preferred
  • Working knowledge of FTP
  • Basic knowledge of SEO Standards and Principals
  • Basic knowledge of Google Analytics and Google Search Console
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, set priorities, utilize resources and meet deadlines
  • Meticulous attention to detail
  • Impeccable knowledge of grammar and punctuation
  • Proven record of Integrity
  • Proven record of following through on commitments
  • Exceptional written and verbal communications skills
  • Quick learner
  • High level of enthusiasm
  • Proven problem solver that takes initiative
  • Result oriented with a strong work ethic
  • Delivers fanatical customer service
  • Proven team player mentality
  • Experience in technology/consumer electronics/home automation industries preferred, but not required

About One Firefly:

One Firefly is an award-winning marketing agency that caters to custom integration and security professionals including designers, integrators, installers, contractors, consultants, distributors, and manufacturers. One Firefly is headquartered in Davie, Florida and has been operating since 2007. One Firefly clients span the globe.
